Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Recipe

This recipe is one of my favorite durong any holiday season. its always so easy to make and is usually the biggest hit at the dinner table. I love making it every year for my family sonce it tastes good and it so easy to make

Ingredients

  1. 2 cans of sweet potatos
  2. 1/4 cup of melted butter
  3. 1/4 cup brown sugar "add to liking after"
  4. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  5. 2 cups of mini marshmellows "enough to cover the entire pan

Supplies

  1. Hand mixer
  2. Bowl
  3. 8x8 or 9x13 dish
  • Preheat your oven to 350 degree F. and spray a 8x8 or 9x13 dish with cooking spray
  • Then in a large bowl add in your sweet potatos, butter, brown sugar, cinnamon
  • Mix with hand mixer until everything is combined then spread mixture in your prepared sprayed pan
  • Put in oven for about 10 minutes to warm it up
  • Once warm take out of the oven and evenly spread your marshmellows across "dont add to many they expand in oven"
  • When the Marshmellows turn golden brown pull it out of the oven and let it cool
  • When its warm its time to enjoy!
